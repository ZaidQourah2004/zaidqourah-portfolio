## Overview

Developed an immersive Star Trek-themed adventure game that combines interactive storytelling with space exploration and strategic decision-making. The game allows players to experience the Star Trek universe firsthand as they navigate through various missions and scenarios.

## Key Features

- **Immersive Storytelling**: Rich narrative experience with branching storylines that adapt to player choices.
- **Ship Command System**: Players can manage their starship, make tactical decisions, and navigate through space.
- **Character Interactions**: Dynamic dialogue system with crew members and alien species.
- **Mission-based Gameplay**: Various missions inspired by classic Star Trek scenarios and moral dilemmas.
- **Space Exploration**: Procedurally generated star systems to explore with unique planets and phenomena.
- **Resource Management**: Strategic allocation of ship resources during critical situations.

## Technologies Used

- **Unity Engine**: Core game development platform for creating the 3D environment.
- **C#**: Primary programming language for game mechanics and systems.
- **Blender**: For 3D modeling of spaceships, characters, and environments.
- **Python**: Used for procedural generation algorithms and data processing.
- **Adobe Creative Suite**: For creating visual assets and UI elements.

## Challenges and Learnings

Creating an authentic Star Trek experience while maintaining engaging gameplay required balancing narrative depth with player agency. Implementing a complex dialogue system that remembered player choices across the game was particularly challenging. The project provided valuable experience in narrative design, game balancing, and creating AI-driven non-player characters that responded realistically to player decisions.

## Outcome

The game has been well-received by both Star Trek fans and casual gamers, praised for its authentic representation of the franchise's themes and its engaging gameplay mechanics. It has fostered a small but dedicated community that continues to provide feedback for ongoing improvements and potential expansions.
